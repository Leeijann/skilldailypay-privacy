SkillDailyPay — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

SkillDailyPay ("we", "our", or "us") is a digital media and content management company operated by Silix LLC. We operate the website skilldailypay.com and the SkillDailyPay Agency application (collectively, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you interact with our Service, including our integrations with third-party platforms.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Name and email address when you create an account or subscribe to our services
  • Social media account information when you authorize connections to platforms such as Pinterest, YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, X (Twitter), LinkedIn, and TikTok
  • Content you create or submit through our Service
  • Communications you send to us (support requests, feedback)

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data such as pages visited, features used, and interaction timestamps
  • Device information including browser type, operating system, and IP address
  • Analytics data from connected social media platforms related to your own account performance

1.3 Information from Third-Party Platforms

When you connect your social media accounts (including Pinterest, YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, X, LinkedIn, and TikTok), we access data through their official APIs strictly in accordance with each platform's terms of service and developer guidelines. We only request the minimum permissions necessary to provide our content management services to you.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our Service
  • Publish and manage content on your connected social media platforms on your behalf
  • Analyze your own account's content performance and provide you with insights
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our Service
  • Communicate with you about your account, updates, and support
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Pinterest-Specific Data Practices

In compliance with Pinterest's Developer Guidelines and API Terms of Service, we adhere to the following practices:

  • No Data Storage: We do not permanently store Pinterest user data obtained through the Pinterest API. Except for campaign analytics about your own account, we call the Pinterest API each time data access is needed rather than retaining copies.
  • Limited Use: Information accessed from your Pinterest account is used solely to provide our content management services directly to you, the account holder.
  • No Data Combining: We do not combine your Pinterest account information with data from other users, nor do we merge Pinterest data with information from other third-party services for profiling or advertising purposes.
  • No Selling or Sharing: We do not sell, distribute, or share your Pinterest data with any third parties, including advertising networks or data brokers. Your Pinterest data is only accessible to you through our Service.
  • Secure Credentials: All Pinterest API access credentials are stored securely using industry-standard encryption and are never exposed, shared, or used by unauthorized parties.
  • User Consent: We access your Pinterest account only after you have explicitly authorized the connection. You may revoke this access at any time.

4. Data Practices for Other Platforms

We apply the same rigorous data handling standards to all connected platforms:

  • YouTube — Data accessed via the YouTube API Services. We comply with YouTube's Terms of Service and the Google Privacy Policy.
  • Facebook & Instagram — Data accessed via the Meta Graph API in compliance with the Meta Platform Terms.
  • X (Twitter) — Data accessed in compliance with the X Developer Agreement.
  • LinkedIn — Data accessed in compliance with LinkedIn API Terms of Use.
  • TikTok — Data accessed in compliance with TikTok's API Terms.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption of data in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest
  • API credentials and access tokens are stored using secure encryption and environment variable management
  • Access to systems containing personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • We conduct regular security reviews and monitoring of our systems
  • We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise share your personal information with third parties for their own purposes. We may disclose information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With your explicit, informed consent
  • To comply with applicable law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request
  • To enforce our terms of service and protect our legal rights
  • To protect the safety and security of our users and the public

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data from our systems
  • Revoke Access: Disconnect any connected social media account at any time through your account settings or by contacting us
  • Opt Out: Opt out of non-essential data collection and communications
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable, machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the information provided below.

8. Cookies and Tracking

Our Service may use cookies and similar technologies to improve functionality and user experience. These include:

  • Essential cookies: Required for basic Service functionality
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our Service

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain features of our Service.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to individuals under the age of 13 (or 16 in the European Economic Area). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected data from a child, we will take immediate steps to delete that information.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in the United States or other jurisdictions where our service providers operate. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for any international data transfers in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act, including the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete your data, and the right to opt out of the sale of your personal information. We do not sell personal information.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy on this page and revising the "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the Service after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
